## RESUME FORMATTING NOTES

### When Exporting to PDF:

1. **Use a clean, professional font:** Calibri, Arial, or Helvetica (10-12pt body, 14-16pt headers)
2. **Keep it to 1-2 pages maximum** — Recruiters spend ~7 seconds on initial scan
3. **Use consistent formatting:** Same bullet style, same heading hierarchy throughout
4. **Save as PDF with descriptive filename:** `FirstName_LastName_AI_Technical_Writer_Resume.pdf`
5. **Ensure all links are clickable** in the PDF version

### ATS Optimization:

- Include keywords from job descriptions: "API documentation," "technical writer," "developer documentation," "machine learning," "LLM," "RAG"
- Use standard section headings ("Professional Experience," "Education," "Skills")
- Avoid graphics, images, or complex formatting that ATS can't parse
- Include both full words and abbreviations: "Large Language Models (LLMs)"

### Tailoring for Each Application:

1. **Read the job description carefully** — Identify key requirements
2. **Adjust Professional Summary** — Mirror language from job posting
3. **Reorder Skills** — Put most relevant skills first in each category
4. **Highlight Relevant Projects** — Move most applicable portfolio piece to top
5. **Add Keywords** — Include specific tools/technologies mentioned in posting
